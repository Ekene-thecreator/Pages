# Privacy Policy
**Last Updated: March 2025**

Welcome to the Food Scanner App (“we”, “our”, or “the App”).  
This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect
### Information You Provide
- Email address (only if you contact support)

### Automatically Collected Data
- Device type and OS  
- Non-identifiable usage statistics  
- Crash logs  

### Product Scan Data
- Barcodes  
- Product metadata from open databases  

We do **not** store personal data from scans.

---

## 2. How We Use Information
- Provide app functionality  
- Improve features and performance  
- Respond to support requests  

We **do not** sell personal information.

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security
- Product data may be cached locally on your device  
- Support emails are securely stored  
- Reasonable protections are used to safeguard data  

---

## 4. Third-Party Services
The App may interact with:
- Open Food Facts  
- GoUPC  
- AWS services  

Only **non-personal** data is shared with these services.
